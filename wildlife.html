Yala Natonal Park

BOOK A TOUR

Yala national park is one of the most popular tourist attractions in sri lanka. This park is considered a national heritage site . It is a home to many animal speacies but mainly to elephants , peacocks , deers and bears. Local and international tourists both can enjoy the sight of these animals safely through safari rides .





sinharaja rainforest

BOOK A TOUR

sinharaja is tropical rainforest located in the southern province of sri lanka. This rainforest is host to of endemic trees ,endemic animal species and of endemic birds. Sinharaja rainforest was declared a world Biosphere Reserve and a world heritage site . Visit this rainforest if you love nature.







Ridiyagama Park

BOOK A TOUR

Ridiyagama is a national park with a 500 acre land area. there are 22 species of animals found in this national park. like lions , deers and peacocks. You can enjoy the site of these magnificant animals for cheap prices at ridiyagama.
