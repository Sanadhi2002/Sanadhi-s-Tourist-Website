Festivals

Sri lanka is a country that celebrates many festivals .We celebrate many festivals a year. These are religious and non- religious festivals . Diwali , Christmas and ramazaan are some of the religious festivals that are celebrated in sri lanka. Sri lankans get together with their family and friends to celebrate these festivals . In April we celebrate the Sinhala - Tamil New year festival, where we play avurudhu games and eat avurudhu "kavili" with our family and friends.In March we celebrate Maha Shivarathri , to remember the marriage of Shiva to Parvati. This festival is celebrated by prayers, worships and parties .The Kandy Esala Perehera is celebrated to pay respect to the sacred toth relic of buddha and is one of the most important buddhist festivals in Sri Lanka. This festival is a week long and happens in July or August

Some of these festivals are unique only to Sri Lanka like the Sinhala Tamil New Year Celebrations. and some are global celebrations like christmas and holi festival.

  • Sinhala - Tamil New year
  • Maha Shivarathri
  • The Kandy Esala Perehera

Historical sites

Sri lanka has a vast history that spreads for far more than 4000 years. This magnificant history can be witnessed through the ancient temples,buildings, relics and cities that are still preserved to this date in Sri Lanka. Anuradhapura , the first capital of Sri Lanka, Polonnaruwa , Sigiriya , the fortress built by King Kashyapa and Galle are some of the locations that are significant when it comes to Sri Lankan history.The kings that ruled Ceylon a thousand years ago. They were concerned with improving the water technology those days which can be seen through the sigiriy fortress . The water fountains in Sigiriya are suprising and show high level thinking and skills of our ancestors.

The Galle District showcases the Portuguese Rule over Sri Lanka . The houses , restaurants found in Galle still shows the Portuguese building structure which adds to the scenery of Galle. Galle is must see location , when it comes to Sri Lanka.

Food and Wine

sri lanka has a vast euthentic cuisine. The main food of Sri Lankans is rice. rice is always a part of the daily diet of Sri Lankans. Sri Lankans enjoy eating greens , herbs and lentils and these are always a part of their daily diet . Some of the popular dishes that Sri Lankans eat are , dhal , sprats ,Pumpkin, Jackfruit and mango. Sri Lankans use spices and herbs to cook their food . Curry powder , chilli powder and black pepper are some of the common spices that are used to cook food by Sri Lankans.
