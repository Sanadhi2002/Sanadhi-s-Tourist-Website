arugam bay



This beach found in the east coast, is known for having the best waves in Sri Lanka. It is a popular beach for surfing and has hosted many international surfing competitions.

Mirissa


This beach is found in the south east coast of Sri Lanka is one of the top tourist attractions of Sri Lanka. Some of the activities in mirissa are whale watching, scuba diving , sun bathing, visiting welligma town and even shopping in matara.

Unawatuna


This beach is one of the top tourist destinations in Sri Lanka due to the presence of coral in the beach. Unwatuna beach is found in the Galle district. You can enjoy yourself by snorkelling , surfing , visiting the turtle rescue sanctury or just relaxing at the beach !!
